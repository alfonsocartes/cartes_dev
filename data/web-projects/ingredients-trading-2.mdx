# Ingredients Trading 71

[Ingredients Trading 71](https://ingredients71.com) is a company wordpress website for a medium business in the food flavouring industry.

Ingredients Trading 71 is a Venezuelan Flavours company in the food industry that supplies food ingredients to the local markets.

The website has a focus on SEO, and for some time, it also provided an e-commerce store using the Woocommerce plugin.

## Technologies

- WordPress
- Woocommerce (disabled at the moment)
- Cloudflare CDN
- LAMP Stack
- Linux Server
- Yoast SEO
- Social Network Connections
